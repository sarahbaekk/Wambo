How It Works

  1. Explore Events: Check out local walks, park meetups, adoption days, and more.
  2. RSVP Easily: Join directly through your Wambo profile—free or low-cost options available.
  3. Show Up & Shine: Bring your pet, your leash, and your good vibes. We’ll take care of the rest.

Why Attend a Wambo Event?

  • ✔ Foster socialization for you and your pet
  • ✔ Support a woman-led, safe space for all participants
